Remove Duplicate Rows

This tool removes rows where all values across all columns are identical to another row in the file.

For example, if your CSV has multiple identical rows like:

Campaign Name,Campaign Type,Vendor
123 Greeting,Email - 1st Party,Rainmaker
123 Greeting,Email - 1st Party,Rainmaker
123 Greeting,Email - 1st Party,Rainmaker

It will be reduced to a single row:

Campaign Name,Campaign Type,Vendor
123 Greeting,Email - 1st Party,Rainmaker

Follow these steps:

  1. Upload your CSV file using the button below
  2. Click "Process CSV" to remove duplicate rows
  3. Download the processed file

Normalize Sponsorship & Newsletter Text

This tool normalizes "Sponsorship" and "Newsletter" text in the Campaign Name column.

It standardizes various forms of these terms to a consistent format with parentheses.

Sponsorship Normalization

The following terms will be normalized to (Sponsorship):

  • Sponsorship
  • sponsorship
  • Sponsor
  • sponsor

Example:

Campaign Name
Acme Email - Sponsorship
Acme Email - Spon
Acme Email - Spons
Acme Email [Sponsorship]

Will be normalized to:

Campaign Name
Acme Email (Sponsorship)
Acme Email (Sponsorship)
Acme Email (Sponsorship)
Acme Email (Sponsorship)

Newsletter Normalization

The following terms will be normalized to (Newsletter):

  • Newsletter
  • newsletter

Example:

Campaign Name
Disney Email - Newsletter
Disney Email - newsletter
Disney Email | Newsletter
Disney Email [Newsletter]

Will be normalized to:

Campaign Name
Disney Email (Newsletter)
Disney Email (Newsletter)
Disney Email (Newsletter)
Disney Email (Newsletter)

Follow these steps:

  1. Upload your CSV file using the button below
  2. Click "Process CSV" to normalize the text
  3. Download the processed file
